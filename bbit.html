BUSSINESS INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY

Introduction

The program equips graduates with both business management skills and information technology. It combines the key aspects of Information Technology (covering areas such as software development, communications and networking, security systems analysis, database, etc.) with key facets of business such as accounting, operations management, organization behavior, etc. This combination of skills and knowledge prepares graduates to be immediately productive in the modern work environment.

Course Objective

The BBIT course is intended to produce Information Communication & Technology (ICT) graduates with requisite management and business skills capable of managing ICT organizations and ICT departments, either in employment or in self employment. The program offers students knowledge and skills in the application of modern information technology within business. It builds skills in computer programming, business problem analysis, application design and development and implementation of IT solutions to business problems.

EMPLOYMENT VENUE

  1. IT Consultant/Manager
  2. Web Developer
  3. Computer Systems Analyst
  4. Software Engineer
  5. Data Modeler
  6. Business Operations Analyst
  7. Project Manager

Course Duration

The degree course shall be offered in 8 academic semesters organized in four academic years. There shall be an industrial attachment at the end of year three. The program will therefore run for a maximum of eight semesters

Requirements

The following shall be eligible for admission considerations, into the programme:

1.Kenya Certificate of Secondary Education (KCSE) applicants with a mean aggregate of at least C+ and at least: 

  • C+ in both Mathematics and English OR
  • Credit in University Bridging Mathematics and C+ in English.
2. Kenya Advanced Certificate of Education (KACE) or A-level applicants with at least 2 principal passes, including Mathematics.
3. Holders of Diploma in Applied Sciences with at least a credit pass in relevant subjects from an institution recognized by the University Senate.
4. A holder of other qualifications recognized by the University Senate as equivalent to the 1 and 2 above.

Students with either qualification 1 or 3 may at the discretion of the Facuality of Science be considered for credit transfer.

Fees

For Kenyan citizens, tuition fees is Kshs.76,403.00 per semester in the first year, and ranges from Kshs. 57,303.00 to Kshs. 62,078.00 for all other semesters. For non citizens, add an extra 20% to the amount in every semester.
